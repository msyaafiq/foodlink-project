About FoodLink

At FoodLink, we are committed to revolutionizing the way we connect surplus food to communities in need. Our mission is to ensure that no one goes hungry, and we're leveraging cutting-edge technology to make this a reality.

Read More

Our Vision

Our vision is simple yet powerful: a world where every individual has access to nutritious food, and where food waste becomes a thing of the past. We believe in the transformative power of technology to make this vision a reality.

What We Do?

FoodLink is an innovative platform that seamlessly connects food donors with recipients. Through the integration of Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), we have developed a system that streamlines food bank resource management. This approach ensures that surplus food reaches those who need it efficiently and reduces food waste.

Our Impact

Our work goes beyond just reducing food waste. FoodLink plays a crucial role in improving food security, supporting local communities, and promoting a sense of social responsibility. We align with government initiatives to reduce hunger, strengthen the economy, and provide valuable data for academic research on food security.

Get Involved

We invite you to join us on this mission. Whether you're a food donor, a recipient, or simply someone who cares about making a difference, there's a place for you in the FoodLink community.
